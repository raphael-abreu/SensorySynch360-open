Informed Consent Form

You are invited to participate as a volunteer in the evaluation of an authoring system called SensorySynch360, which was developed as part of research by student Raphael Abreu for the postgraduate program at UFF.

OBJECTIVE: The main purpose of this study is to evaluate the user experience of the system.
PROCEDURES:
  • You will perform actions in the system to annotate videos with sensory effects.
  • We will also present 3 questionnaires for you to evaluate your experience with the system.
  • Remember, your answers will not be used to evaluate you. You can cancel the test at any time by closing this window.
  • The entire study will take no more than 50 minutes.
IDENTITY CONFIDENTIALITY:
  • No questions will be asked about your identity.
  • By accepting to participate, you will receive a unique identifier to be used in the study. This identifier will be displayed at the top of the page.
  • The data obtained in the study is confidential and will not be disclosed at an individual level.

How to perform the tasks:

1) As soon as you open the system, you must click on the [Start test] button to start the task and a video will be loaded automatically

2) Hover over the button in the middle of the screen labeled [Task 1] to read the test description.

Attention: Various sensory effects may be necessary for a complete experience, but it doesn't need to be too detailed! Only annotate according to what the task requires.

3) Click the [Finish test] button to finish. Please: after [Finish test], wait for a confirmation message before closing the test tab

Stage 2

In this phase, you will use a new feature! The automatic extraction of sensory effects.

The extraction uses a combination of artificial intelligence modules to identify types, locations, and moments of activation of the effects

The goal is not for it to be fully automated, but for the automatic extraction to give a kickstart to authoring. Some refinement may be necessary
